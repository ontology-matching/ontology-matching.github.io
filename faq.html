Q: I have relevant to Ontology Matching resources, which are not mentioned on this web site, but, as I think, they should be. How do I submit a request?

A: Please send your requests to Pavel Shvaiko at pavel.shvaiko [at] tndigit.it


Q: What type of materials is included into the Publication section?

A: There are three types of materials that are included into the Publication section: 1 - archival publications, 2 - workshop proceedings and special issues in journals, 3 - deliverables/technical reports from big projects, e.g., FP7, H2020.
